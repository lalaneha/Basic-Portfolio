About me

I was born in Sunnyside Queens, New York City, NY to two amazing Indian parents. My family n I moved to WA State when I was 1 years old. I love living here in the Pacific Northwest because I enjoy hiking while checking out the breath-taking views! Some of my favorite hikes are in the North Bend area, Rattlesnake ledge and Little Mount Si.

My favorite sports are soccer and volleyball and my favorite person to play with is my little brother!

My parents being both of Indian nationality, I love cooking and eating Indian foods! Some of my favorite Indian food dishes are vegetable biryani and paneer tika masala!
